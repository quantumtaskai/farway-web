ABOUT FAR WAY COMPANY

Who We Are

Established in 2001, Far Way has evolved into a premier global FMCG distribution powerhouse, connecting world-class brands with markets across 40+ countries.

Our Legacy

With over 23 years of expertise in the FMCG sector, Far Way has built an unparalleled reputation for excellence, innovation, and reliability in global distribution networks.

From our strategic headquarters in the dynamic city of Dubai, UAE, we've expanded our footprint to serve diverse markets worldwide, establishing ourselves as a trusted bridge between premium brands and discerning consumers through our comprehensive FMCG Import/Export, Distribution & Shipping services.

Our Mission

To connect exceptional global brands with markets worldwide through innovative distribution solutions and unwavering commitment to quality.

Our Vision

To be the world's most trusted FMCG distribution partner, setting global standards for excellence, sustainability, and innovation.
